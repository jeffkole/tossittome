How to toss

Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmark bar: TossIt Then click "TossIt" when you're on a page you want to toss to another computer.

From an iOS mobile device, follow these steps:

  1. Click this link: TossIt.
  2. Tap the share button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap "Bookmark" and then "Save".
  4. Tap the bookmarks button at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Click the "Edit" link at the bottom.
  6. Select the bookmark you just added.
  7. Delete all of the text from the address before the word "javascript": http://tossitto.me/#.

  8. Click "Done" on the keyboard.

How to catch

Pages are caught by a Chrome extension. Here's how to install it:

  1. Download the extension. (when Chrome objects, click "Ok").
  2. In Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions.
  3. Drag "tossittome-{{ manifest.version }}.crx" to the tab with the extensions page open.
  4. Grant the permissions.

Happy tossing.
