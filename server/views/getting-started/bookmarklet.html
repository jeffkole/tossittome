How to toss

  • Desktop
  • Mobile Safari
  • Mobile Chrome
  1. Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmark bar: TossIt.
  2. Click “TossIt” when you’re on a page you want to toss to another computer.

In Safari on an iOS mobile device, follow these steps:

Click this link: TossIt.

Tap the share button at the bottom of the screen.

Tap “Bookmark” and then “Save”

Tap the bookmarks button at the bottom of the screen, and then the “Edit” link at the bottom.

Select the bookmark you just added.

Delete all of the text from the address before the word “javascript”.
http://tossitto.me/getting-started/bookmarklet#

Edit “Toss It To Me!” to “Toss”, and click “Done” on the keyboard.

From a page you want to toss, touch the bookmarks button at the bottom of the screen, and then tap the “Toss” bookmarklet.

In Chrome on a mobile device, follow these steps:

Click this link: TossIt.

Tap the menu icon, and then the star.

Edit “Toss It To Me!” to “Toss”.

Delete all of the text from the address before the word “javascript”.
http://tossitto.me/getting-started/bookmarklet#

Click “Save”.

From a page you want to toss, touch the address bar, type “toss” and tap the menu item that shows up with a star to its left.
